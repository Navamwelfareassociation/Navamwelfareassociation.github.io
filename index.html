About Us

VISION


“To create a platform to uplift the standard of deserving one, to provide the opportunity to learn, develop and transform into a well-rounded individual by helping them to enhance their talents”



Objectives


  • Navam works for the development of Tamil Language.
  • Navam aims to create awareness on the importance of Voting among people.
  • Navam provide foods periodically to orphanage people, old aged people and homeless people.
  • Navam encourages Natural Agriculture among people.
  • Navam aims to eliminate Untouchability.
